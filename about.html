Strategic Technology Consulting

WeMake, Corp. is a technology-oriented strategic consulting firm headquartered in San Diego, California. We serve enterprises, research institutions, and government partners who need trusted advisors at the intersection of science, engineering, and commercial strategy.

Our consultants include distinguished professors, seasoned industry executives, and hands-on technology innovators with proven track records in biotherapeutics, advanced propulsion technology, AI application and integration, advanced robotics, neuromodulation, and regenerative therapies.

We do not simply recommend—we partner. From regulatory strategy and clinical development to technology deployment and scale, WeMake delivers comprehensive, future-proof consulting solutions tailored to each client's mission.
